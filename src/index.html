How to calculate FIRE

The Financial Independence, Retire Early (FIRE) movement is a movement of young people working hard and saving as much as possible with one goal in mind: To save enough money that they can retire before the traditional retirement age.

Retirement = Annual retirement spending / SWR

Networth time-series: Last Networth * Expected Return + (Take Home Pay - Annual Spending)
